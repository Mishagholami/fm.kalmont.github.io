About Us

Kalmont Mountain Safety Resources

Our Mission

At Kalmont, our mission is to empower outdoor enthusiasts with the knowledge and skills needed to safely enjoy mountain environments. We believe that education is the foundation of wilderness safety, and we are committed to making this education accessible to all.

Through our comprehensive training programs, research initiatives, and community outreach, we strive to reduce wilderness accidents and foster a culture of responsible outdoor recreation. Our team of experienced mountaineers, wilderness first responders, and outdoor educators brings decades of combined expertise to our programs.

Who We Are

Kalmont was founded in 2005 by a group of mountain guides and wilderness educators who recognized the need for specialized safety training in alpine environments. What began as a small training center has grown into a comprehensive resource for mountain safety education, research, and advocacy.

Our team includes certified mountain guides, avalanche specialists, wilderness medicine instructors, and environmental scientists. This diverse expertise allows us to offer holistic safety education that considers not only technical skills but also environmental factors and decision-making processes.

What We Do

Kalmont offers a comprehensive range of services designed to enhance mountain safety:

  • Training Programs: From introductory wilderness safety courses to advanced technical training, our curriculum covers all aspects of mountain safety.
  • Research: We conduct and publish research on mountain hazards, equipment effectiveness, and accident prevention strategies.
  • Resource Development: Our team creates educational materials, safety guidelines, and decision-making tools for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Consulting: We provide expert consultation to outdoor organizations, parks, and recreation areas on safety protocols and risk management.
  • Community Outreach: Through workshops, events, and partnerships, we work to build a culture of safety within the outdoor community.
